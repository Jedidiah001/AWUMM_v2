What is the Controversy Free Agent Pool?

These are free agents who are currently unemployed because of a public incident — things like a failed drug test, legal trouble, a social media scandal, or a backstage incident. No other promotion has signed them because of the risk involved.

You can choose to sign them at a discounted salary, but there is a risk. Each card below shows you whether the risk is worth taking. Click any card to see the full breakdown and your signing options.
